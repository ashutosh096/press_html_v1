Four Story Angles
01

The Farmer's Reality

Farmers in Moth, Bamaur, and Gursarai are drilling deeper wells each year. This report puts 123 years of data behind what they have been saying — and shows it is getting worse, not better.

02

The Heat Story

Jhansi has one of the steepest rising heat-extreme trends in Uttar Pradesh. The same blocks losing rain are the ones whose heat extremes are intensifying — a compounding pressure on outdoor workers, crops, and agricultural credit risk.

03

The Watershed & Water Demand Story

Pre-monsoon is peak agricultural and domestic water demand — precisely when rainfall is lowest and temperatures highest. Watershed investments and rural credit portfolios designed around old rainfall patterns face a growing mismatch with current conditions.

04

The Planning Gap

Water systems and rural programmes in Jhansi were built around historical climate norms. Block-level data now exists to inform where interventions are most urgent — and where existing schemes need to be reassessed.
